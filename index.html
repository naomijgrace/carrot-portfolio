About Me

My journey in architecture began with a deep-seated passion for design, which led me to pursue a Diploma of Design and Architecture. This initial step ignited my fascination with architectural development and set the stage for my academic journey. Building on this foundation, I completed a Bachelor of Design in Architecture, where my enthusiasm for the architectural process truly flourished. During my university years, I actively participated in all end-of-year parodies and final presentations, embracing opportunities to showcase and refine my design concepts. These experiences not only honed my technical skills but also underscored my commitment to collaborative and innovative design. My academic achievements, marked by engaging project work and effective presentations, reflect my dedication to transforming ideas into impactful architectural solutions and my ongoing love for the intricate process of project development.
